About

GOVAID is a Platform that aims to offer one-stop search and discovery of the Government schemes.

It provides an innovative, technology-based solution to discover scheme information based upon the eligibility of the citizen.

The platform helps the citizen to find the right Government schemes for them. It also guides on how to apply for different Government schemes. Thus no need to visit multiple Government websites.

What is GOVAID?▼

GOVAID is an e-Marketplace for Govt. schemes and services. Using GOVAID you can find various government schemes, check your eligibility, and apply for the schemes.

How GOVAID will help common citizens?▼

GOVAID will reduce the time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes.The easy user interface of GOVAID helps common people a lot in discovering and applying for schemes.

Can I apply for the schemes through GOVAID?▼

For now, the platform shall direct you to the application page of the scheme of your choice.It is envisaged that in the upcoming phases, GOVAID shall have the feature to apply for schemes / services from within the platform/ app.

How does GOVAID How do I check for my eligibility through GOVAID?▼

GOVAID offers a convenient three - step process for the citizens to discover the government schemes for which they are eligible:
Step 1 - The user enters his / her attributes such as demographic, income, social details, etc.
Step 2 - The GOVAID findsthe relevant schemes from hundreds of schemes for the user.
Step 3 - The user can select from the list of eligible schemes and get more information from the dedicated scheme page with detailed knowledge, FAQs, and application process and traverse to the application URL to apply.
